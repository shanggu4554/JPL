ABOUT US

HAFFEE COFFEE SHOP

Welcome to Haffee Coffee Shop! We are a small, family-owned business located in the heart of Quezon City, dedicated to serving the best coffee in town. Our passion for coffee started years ago, and we wanted to share that love with our community by opening up our own coffee shop. At Haffee Coffee Shop, we pride ourselves on using only the highest quality coffee beans from around the world, carefully selected and roasted to perfection. We offer a variety of coffee drinks, from classic espresso to specialty lattes and cappuccinos, as well as teas and other refreshing beverages. We believe that a great cup of coffee is only part of the equation. That's why we've worked hard to create a warm and inviting atmosphere in our shop, where our customers can relax and enjoy their coffee. We want our shop to feel like a second home for our customers, and we strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels comfortable. At Haffee Coffee Shop, we are committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing practices. We work directly with coffee farmers to ensure that they are paid fairly for their crops and that they are able to maintain sustainable farming practices. We also use eco-friendly materials and processes whenever possible, to minimize our impact on the environment. Thank you for choosing Haffee Coffee Shop. We look forward to serving you the best cup of coffee you've ever had!
